About Us

----------

Overlooking the historic Grand Canal, The Hilton Dublin is a 10-minute walk from the famous St. Stephen's Green and Grafton Street. Underground parking is available. Air-conditioned rooms have power showers and TVs. The Hilton has multi-lingual staff and offers 24-hour room service. Bedrooms have large opening windows and provide mini-bars, hair dryers and irons. At Charlemont Bar & Bistro, dining options include a hearty breakfast or nutritious light bite; dishes produced with fresh ingredients, or guests can relax with a hot drink in the Charlemont Bar & Bistro. The Charlemont Luas stop is outside the hotel and provides easy access to South Dublin, including the fashionable Dundrum shopping centre. Temple Bar is just a 15-minute walk. The Fitness by Precor Gym has a wide range of exercises machines, all with personal audio visual units.
